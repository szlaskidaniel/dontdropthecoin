## Description (App Store)

A glass vessel full of precious diamonds — buried under junk. Bolts, paperclips, teddy bears, donuts… the clutter never ends. Tilt your phone, use real-world gravity to sift out the trash — and keep every sparkling gem safely inside.

Sift is a physics-based puzzle game that blends quick reflexes with careful strategy. Each round fills a glass vessel with glowing crystals and a growing pile of junk. Tilt to coax the trash over the rim — but one wrong move and your diamonds spill out with it.

EASY TO LEARN, IMPOSSIBLE TO MASTER
Pure tilt controls — no buttons, no tapping. Just you, gravity, and the laws of physics.

ESCALATING CHALLENGE
New junk types unlock as you progress: heavy rocks, slippery paperclips, stubborn bolts, and floating balloons that push crystals around. Sticky poop glues objects together — shake to break free. Fragile bombs detonate after three hard strikes against the glass.

FIVE UNIQUE VESSELS
Classic bottle, Erlenmeyer flask, round-bottom flask, tall flask, and wide-open beaker — each with its own physics. The vessel changes every five stages, forcing you to adapt.

GORGEOUS VISUALS
Faceted gem shaders in cyan, amber, ruby, and emerald. Realistic glass lighting, parallax bokeh background, and delicate crystal clink sound effects.

SCORING & STATS
Preserve every diamond for a Perfect Stage bonus. Track lifetime stats: diamonds saved, junk sorted, and more.
